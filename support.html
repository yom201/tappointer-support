Common Checks

  • Imported videos can be up to 10 minutes long. If a video is longer, trim it in Photos or another app before importing it.
  • Microphone permission is required only when recording a voiceover.
  • Photos add-only permission is required when saving an exported video to the Photos app.
  • If a purchased export unlock is not reflected, try Restore Purchase in the app.
